Burger

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Bun
  • Lettuce, tomato
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Shape into patties and grill or fry.
  3. Toast the bun.
  4. Assemble with veggies and cheese.

Nutrition:

  • Calories: 250–300 kcal
  • Protein: 17–20g
  • Fat: 10–15g
  • Carbs: 25–30g
